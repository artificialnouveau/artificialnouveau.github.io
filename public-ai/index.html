Public AI Creative Fellowship

MetaGov — Creative Technologist Fellow — 2025–2026

The Public AI Creative Fellowship is a six-month initiative by MetaGov that brings together creative professionals to develop cultural narratives around public AI — systems that are open, democratically governed, and accountable to the communities they affect.

The fellowship recruited three fellows — a Video Creator, a Creative Technologist, and a Writer — to produce work that makes the case for AI as public infrastructure. The first phase focused on experimentation with formats and collaborative exploration of narratives. The second phase refined these explorations into interactive, public-facing experiences for broader engagement.

As the Creative Technologist fellow, I developed interactive digital experiences — games, simulators, satirical websites, and surveys — that explore what it means for AI to be publicly owned, governed, and accountable. Each project below takes a different angle on the question of who should control AI and for whose benefit.

Projects

MORE AI! I NEED IT! PLEASE!

A satirical catalog of the most absurd, questionable, and bewilderingly real AI products flooding the market. Part consumer guide, part cultural critique — featuring tiered pricing models, a usefulness-vs-desirability rating chart, and a mystery box that randomly assigns you an AI service.

SATIRE INTERACTIVE

Airbus for AI

What if middle powers merged their sovereign AI companies into a Public AI Company? An interactive simulator based on the Bennett School analysis that lets you assemble national AI champions, run battle scenarios, and explore a 10-year roadmap for a publicly governed AI mega-corporation.

SIMULATOR POLICY

AI Perspectives Survey

An interactive survey that maps your views on AI onto an archetype spectrum — from AI accelerationists who want to hit the gas pedal, to cautious optimists tapping the brakes, to those who see AI as an existential threat. See how your perspective compares to others.

SURVEY INTERACTIVE

AI Governance Simulator

A retro-styled strategy game where you navigate the complexities of AI governance. Make policy decisions, balance competing interests, and see how your choices shape the future of AI regulation and public accountability.
