ELIGIBILITY: US-based or US-territory 501(c)(3) organizations or fiscally sponsored projects; annual operating budget generally under $2 million; grassroots organizations or coalitions rooted in and accountable to impacted communities, with a clear power-building and community-mobilization strategy. Priority to organizations in underfunded Southern, rural and high-threat geographies and those led by most-impacted communities. Applications are accepted on a ROLLING basis while funding remains available: complete the Just Futures Eligibility Quiz first; eligible organizations receive the Letter of Interest form, and selected applicants are invited to submit a full proposal.
