Open calls

  1. FILE NOT FOUND: Open Call for Archive Residencies

    Goethe-Institut · On-site at ten cultural archives around the world; final exhibition at BLMK Cottbus, Germany · Deadline: 07 Jun 2026 · Award: EUR 4,000 (EUR 2,000 artist fee + EUR 2,000 production budget). Travel and accommodation covered up to EUR 2,000 for one person.

    Open call to participate in FILE NOT FOUND, a project exploring the risks and challenges archives face as sites of cultural memory, resistant knowledge and alternative perspectives under growing political pressure. Ten practitioners - artists, thinkers, activists, theatre makers, dancers, musicians and more - will be selected for residencies at ten cultural archives around the world. On site, residents engage with existing archival practices, explore both safe and not-so-safe spaces, investigate traditional and experimental methods of archiving, and ideally develop artistically subversive approaches that strengthen the resilience of archives against external influence. ELIGIBILITY: artists worldwide, all disciplines. Applicants review the ten participating archives and select the one they wish to apply to. The on-site residency runs 2 weeks to 2 months between August 2026 and May 2027. OUTCOME: an artwork plus a residency report, with a group exhibition at BLMK Cottbus, Germany in autumn 2027. Working language English.

  2. Arte Laguna Prize 2026 (21st Edition)

    Arte Laguna (Venice) · Venice, Italy (finalist exhibition at Arsenale Nord) · Deadline: 30 Jun 2026 · Award: EUR 10,000 main prize plus additional FOMAS prizes (EUR 7,000 / 4,000 / 3,000) and a finalist exhibition at the Venice Arsenale Nord. Entry fee approximately EUR 97 to EUR 122.

    International Arte Laguna Prize, open to artists across many media including dedicated digital art and video sections, with a finalist exhibition at the Venice Arsenale Nord. ELIGIBILITY: individual artists of all ages and nationalities worldwide. NOTE: an entry fee applies (roughly EUR 97 to EUR 122), but the prize awards substantial cash. Apply via the Arte Laguna Prize page.

  3. Beautiful Bizarre Art Prize 2026

    Beautiful Bizarre Magazine · Worldwide (exhibition in San Francisco, USA) · Deadline: 17 Jul 2026 · Award: Grand Prize $10,000 plus category awards (e.g. Imaginative Realism $5,000) and a dedicated Digital Art Award and Emerging Artist Award; $76,000+ total in cash and prizes. NOTE: $40 entry fee per submission.

    International, non-acquisitive art prize across seven categories including a dedicated Digital Art Award, with cash prizes and a San Francisco gallery exhibition. Grand Prize is $10,000, with category awards and over $76,000 in total cash and prizes. ELIGIBILITY: open internationally with no geographic restriction; all static media; includes an Emerging Artist Award. NOTE: there is a $40 entry fee per submission.
