  5. Kulturstiftung des Bundes: General Project Funding (Allgemeine Projektfoerderung)

    Kulturstiftung des Bundes (German Federal Cultural Foundation) · Activity must be visible to audiences in Germany.     Germany's flagship open, non-thematic federal project line, and one of the largest European funders that will back a festival, exhibition programme or multi-partner convening run by an organisation rather than an individual artist. Two practical barriers: the application form must be completed in German, and project communications must include a German-language version.
