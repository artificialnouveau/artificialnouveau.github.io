Sample EXPERIENCE datasets: https://osf.io/suqkp/overview?view_only=fdc73f1485664a2c9c7571e0c62abe6a (paper: 'Exploring the role of micro-valence in the phenomenal space', https://doi.org/10.1093/nc/niag005).
