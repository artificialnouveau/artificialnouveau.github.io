Media researcher, computer vision AI engineer, and computational artist working between New York and the Netherlands. PhD in Medicine from Leiden University. Mozilla Creative Media Award recipient, Processing Foundation Fellow, and Public AI Creative Fellow. Creates research-driven artistic work exposing algorithmic violence and harm emerging from automated decision-making systems.

Recent Projects (2024-2025)

2025 DEEPFAKE: A Video Essay
Critical video essay exploring implications and technology behind deepfake media, examining ethical considerations and societal impacts of synthetic media generation.
2024 Fashion Police Drones
CICA Museum, Seoul, Republic of Korea
Interactive installation transforming drones into arbiters of fashion. Audience sets criteria for "fashion norms" via platform. Custom fashion recognition algorithms track individuals. Exhibited at New Media Arts Conference, Korea.
2024 Screen-to-Soundscape
Supported by Constant, Processing Foundation, Stimulerings Fonds
Free and open-source experimental screen reader transforming browsers into immersive soundscapes. Features spatial audio and rich alt-text for blind and visually impaired users. Awarded Fresh Perspective grant and Processing Foundation Fellowship.
